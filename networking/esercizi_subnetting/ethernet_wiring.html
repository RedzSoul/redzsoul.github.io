Ethernet Wire Order

Drag and drop the colored wires to create either a T-568A or T-568B wire ending. T-568B is the most common type of wiring, but for crossover cables one end will have to be T-568A.
For a crossover cable, pins 1 and 2 are switched with 3 and 6.
